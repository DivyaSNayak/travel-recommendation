About TravelWise

At TravelWise, we're passionate about helping travelers discover the world's most incredible destinations. Founded in 2024, our mission is to provide personalized travel recommendations that match your unique preferences.

🌟 Our Mission

To make travel planning effortless, inspiring, and accessible to everyone. We believe that travel has the power to transform lives and create lasting memories.

📌 How to Use TravelWise:

1. Type a country name in the search bar (e.g., "Italy", "Japan", "Thailand", "Greece")
2. Click the Search button to see beautiful destinations with images
3. Browse through the results showing different countries with photos
4. Use the Reset button to clear your search
5. Navigate between pages using the navigation bar
